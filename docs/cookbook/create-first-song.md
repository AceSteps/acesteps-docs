# Create Your First Song

A complete walkthrough of creating, minting, and publishing your first song on AceSteps.

## Prerequisites

- Farcaster account
- Wallet with ETH on Base (for gas)
- Access to AceSteps Mini App

## Step 1: Open AceSteps

1. Open Warpcast on mobile or desktop
2. Navigate to AceSteps Mini App
3. Connect your wallet when prompted

## Step 2: Write Your Prompt

Navigate to the creation screen and enter a prompt:

```
chill lofi beat with soft piano melodies,
vinyl crackle, and gentle rain sounds
```

### Tips for Better Prompts

- Be specific about genre and mood
- Include 3-5 descriptive elements
- Mention instruments if important
- Keep it under 100 words

## Step 3: Generate Music

1. Click **Generate**
2. Wait for AI to create your song (10-30 seconds)
3. Preview the result

Not happy? Click **Regenerate** to try again.

## Step 4: Save as NFT

Once satisfied:

1. Click **Save**
2. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
3. Wait for minting to complete

Your song is now an NFT in your wallet.

## Step 5: Publish (Optional)

To make your song discoverable:

1. Go to **Library**
2. Select your song
3. Click **Publish**
4. Confirm the transaction

## Step 6: Enable Trading (Optional)

To tokenize and trade your song:

1. Select your published song
2. Click **Enable Trading**
3. Confirm the transaction

Your NFT is now locked and 100,000 tokens are created.
