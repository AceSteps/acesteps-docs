# Welcome to AceSteps

**AceSteps** is an AI-powered music creation and tokenization platform built on Base Network as a Farcaster Mini App.

## What Can You Do?

### Create Music with AI

Turn text prompts into unique music tracks using ACE-Step AI.

```
"upbeat electronic track with synth arpeggios and punchy drums"
```

### Own Your Music

Every song you create becomes an ERC-721 NFT in your wallet.

### Enable Trading

Tokenize your songs to allow fans to invest:

- 100,000 tokens per song
- 80% to creator
- 20% to liquidity pool

### Earn Revenue

Revenue from ads flows to token holders via Uniswap V4.
