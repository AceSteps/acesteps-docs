# Overview

Welcome to **AceSteps** - the AI-powered music creation and tokenization platform built on Base Network.

## What is AceSteps?

AceSteps is a Farcaster Mini App that enables anyone to create, own, and earn from music. The platform solves the music industry's biggest challenges:

### The Problem

**For Creators:**
- Traditional music industry has high barriers to entry
- Record labels take 80-90% of revenue
- Artists need expensive equipment, studios, and years of training
- No direct connection with fans for investment/support

**For Fans:**
- No way to directly invest in favorite artists
- No ownership stake in music they love
- Cannot participate in an artist's success story

### The Solution

AceSteps provides a three-pillar solution:

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **AI Music Creation** | Anyone can create professional-quality music using text prompts - no musical training required |
| **NFT Ownership** | Every song is minted as an ERC-721 NFT - creator owns 100% of the copyright |
| **Fractional Investment** | Songs can be tokenized into 100,000 ERC-20 tokens for fan investment |

## Key Features

### AI Music Generation

Create original, copyright-free music:

```
"chill lofi beat with rain sounds"
"upbeat pop anthem with tropical vibes"
"emotional piano ballad about lost love"
```

- Generation time: ~4.5 seconds for 30 seconds of music
- No skill required - just describe what you want
- 100% original content using ACE-Step AI

### NFT Minting

Every song becomes an ERC-721 NFT:

- Blockchain-verified ownership
- Immutable metadata on IPFS
- Signature-based minting ensures only AI-generated music

### Token Trading

Enable fractional ownership:

- 100,000 tokens per song
- Trade on Uniswap V4
- Revenue automatically increases token value

### Revenue Sharing

Transparent, on-chain revenue distribution:

- 80% of ad revenue to creators/holders
- 20% to platform
- Automatic via Uniswap V4 hooks
