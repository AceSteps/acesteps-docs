# Quickstart

Get started with AceSteps in under 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- A Farcaster account
- A wallet with ETH on Base Network
- Access to the AceSteps Mini App

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Open the AceSteps Mini App on Farcaster and connect your wallet.

```
1. Open Warpcast
2. Navigate to AceSteps Mini App
3. Click "Connect Wallet"
4. Approve the connection
```

## Step 2: Create Your First Song

Enter a prompt to generate AI music:

```
"chill lofi beat with rain sounds and soft piano"
```

## Step 3: Mint as NFT

After previewing your song, click "Save" to mint it as an NFT.

## Step 4: Publish & Trade

1. Go to your Library
2. Select your song
3. Click "Publish" to make it available
4. (Optional) Enable trading to tokenize your song
